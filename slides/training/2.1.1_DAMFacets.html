# Nuxeo training - DAM facets
###Context

What about DAM ?

### DAM facets #### Document types - DAM is meant to store pictures, audio and video files - DAM brings corresponding facets for these doctypes #### Asset - The Asset facet is mandatory for any document that should be searchable in DAM using default search tools #### Pictures - Picture: defines pictures, extracts EXIF and similar metadata - Multiview picture: check it for pictures, for technical reasons (will be merged into the Picture facet) #### Audio - Audio: Shows an audio player, extracts tags from audio files (ex : MP3 tags) #### Video - Video: necessary for video documents - Has video preview: generates a screenshot (requires Video facet) - Has storyboard: adds a panel to reach different moments (requires Video facet)
